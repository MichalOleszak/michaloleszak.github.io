My master's thesis project involved forecasting with a collection of very short time series. The resulting R package is available on CRAN.

Proficient Python and R coder with a profound understanding of statistics and (deep) machine learning algorithms. Experienced with the development, deployment, monitoring and maintenance of complex machine learning systems. Well-versed in Bayesian inference and probabilistic programming. Has worn all the hats, having worked for a consultancy, an AI-startup, and a software house.
